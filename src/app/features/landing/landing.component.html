How it works

■

Store

Your data lives in your pod, not inside apps. Photos, notes, contacts — it all belongs to you.

◊

Connect

Apps ask your pod for permission to read or write. You decide what each app can access — and revoke it any time.

⇄

Switch

Move to a new app any time. Your data comes with you because it was never the app's in the first place.

The old way vs. Solid

Today

  • Each app stores your data in its own silo
  • Switching apps means starting over
  • Your data disappears if the service shuts down
  • You agree to terms you haven't read

With Solid

  • One data home, many apps
  • Switch apps and keep everything
  • Your data is yours, stored where you choose
  • You grant or revoke access at any time
